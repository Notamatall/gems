GAME INFO - Gems Cave

ABOUT THE GAME

Venture deep into the Gems Cave, where glittering stones and hidden treasures await. Match shining gems and crack open rare chests to uncover massive rewards. This all-scatter win game features cascading symbols, a thrilling bonus game, and a max win of up to 10,000× your bet!

Gems Cave is played on a 6-reel, 5-row grid with scatter-based payouts and symbol cascades. Win combinations remove symbols and allow new ones to fall in, offering multiple chances per spin.

FEATURES

Crystal Depths Bonus Game

Land 3 FS Chest symbols to trigger the Crystal Depths bonus feature with 10 free spins.

In this feature:

  • Symbols cascade after every win.
  • Special chest symbols apply win multipliers during the bonus round:
    • Silver Chest → adds +2× multiplier to the round total
    • Golden Chest → adds +3× multiplier
    • Obsidian Chest → adds +4× multiplier
  • All multipliers are applied to the each spin of that free spin.
  • FS Chest symbols can re-trigger the bonus:
    • 2 FS symbols = +2 additional spins
    • 3 FS symbols = +4 additional spins

WAYS TO WIN

Winning rules in the base game apply throughout bonus rounds and free spins, unless otherwise stated. Every wager qualifies for all possible combinations.

To win, land 8 or more matching symbols of the same type anywhere on the grid. For special chest symbols, only 6 or more are needed to trigger a payout.

After each win, the matched symbols are removed, and new ones fall into place. This cascade continues until no further wins are possible.
